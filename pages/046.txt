H: The Simplest Bitcoin Book Ever Written

[b] *Bitcoin is a distributed, decentralized, transparent
and immutable LEDGER OF ACCOUNT.*

[b] Anyone in the world can see who owns what, at any
given moment, and it cannot be changed.

[b] Except the ‘who’ isn’t a name, *it is an address
made up of numbers and letters.*

[obb:

  [b] An example of a bitcoin address:

      *bc1qar0srrr7xfkvy5l643lydnw9re59gtzzwf5mdq*
]

[b] Bitcoin is therefore *pseudonymous.*
