BENEFITS OF ⚡️:
VOLUME - The volume of transactions is essentially unlimited, as multiple channels can be opened at the same time, each with its own 'wallet'.
MICRO TRANSACTIONS - You can send as little as 1 satoshi ($0.0006).
SPEED - It usually takes a few seconds to confirm a transaction.
PRIVACY - Transactions are not stored on the public blockchain. They are much more private than cash, as the other party doesn't need to know who you are, and you can use multiple channels to reach the recipient.
It's important to note that this is not 100% private, as the transactions are still visible on the public blockchain. It's just much more private than cash.
