Induced Ponderings
BITCOIN, NONVIOLENT COMMUNICATION & PERMACULTURE
I see Bitcoin, brought to us by Satoshi Nakamoto, as being the foundation layer for a healthy society with regards to:
communicating value
transacting and exchanging
in an emergent, organic, honest unfolding, storing our time/life energy
I see Nonviolent Communication, brought to us by Marshall Rosenberg PhD, as being the foundation layer for a healthy society with regards to:
expressing feelings and needs
deep listening, empathy
in an emergent, organic, honest unfolding, finding common ground solutions
I see Organic Farming and Permaculture, brought to us by Masunobu Fukuoka and Bill Mollison, as being the foundation layer for a healthy society with regards to:
communicating with the earth
growing food, improving the soil
in an emergent, organic, honest unfolding, protecting the environment
