SECURITY & AVOIDING SCAMS
Stick with bitcoin only.
Be super vigilant about your cyber security!
Do your own research and secure your bitcoin.
Never give your bitcoin seed phrase to anyone!
Do not click on any links in emails asking you to verify your account details. Instead, go directly to the official website and check if there is any transaction pending.
Be aware of scams on social media platforms. If someone randomly messages you, it is likely a scam.
If someone offers you a deal to double your bitcoin, it is likely a scam.
Be aware of scams on YouTube. If you see a video about doubling your bitcoin, it is likely a scam.
BE CERTAIN about the address you are sending your bitcoin to, as transactions are irreversible. Ideally check the whole address, especially if it is a large amount.
