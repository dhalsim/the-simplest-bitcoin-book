How to Buy KYC Bitcoin (with ID):
NOT RECOMMENDED
  1. Download the bitcoin-only wallet app (see pg 102 ).
  2. Select a bitcoin-only exchange.
  3. Create an account and select a payment method.
  4. Complete the KYC verification requirements.
  5. Buy the bitcoin.
  6. Withdraw your bitcoin to your own wallet.
  7. Hold or spend your bitcoin as needed.
Be aware that your bitcoin will be forever linked to your identity if you buy it this way, thus forfeiting future pseudonymity with regard to these purchases.
If you choose this method, I recommend finding a reputable bitcoin-only exchange.
Be sure the exchange allows you to withdraw your bitcoin to your own wallet!
Exchanges are required by law to 'KYC' you.
They will take your full name, address, social security number, email, phone number and often a photo of you holding your ID.
Confirm that the exchange has both phone and email support for customer service.
