BITCOIN CALENDAR OF NOTABLE DAYS
August 18, 2008 ~ The domain name bitcoin.org was registered.
October 31, 2008 ~ Bitcoin Technical Document Day: "Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System" was published on metzdowd.com by an anonymous cryptographer named Satoshi Nakamoto.
January 3, 2009 ~ Bitcoin's Birthday: Satoshi's Genesis block was mined, launching the Bitcoin network.
January 12, 2009 ~ The First Bitcoin Transaction: Satoshi sent 10 bitcoins to Hal Finney, marking the first transaction on the Bitcoin network.
October 5, 2009 ~ The First Bitcoin Exchange: The New Liberty Standard (NLS) was launched on a Bitcoin exchange with a price of 0.00764 USD per Bitcoin.
October 12, 2009 ~ "I found the first known Bitcoin-USD transaction in my email backups. On October 12, 2009, I sold 5.050 bitcoins for $5.02." - Martti Malmi, the founder of NewLibertyStandard, sold bitcoins on the first Bitcoin exchange.
May 22, 2010 ~ Bitcoin Pizza Day: Bitcoin was used for the first time to purchase a good or service, when Lazslo Hanyecz bought two Papa John's pizzas for 10,000 bitcoins.
December 12, 2010 ~ This is the date Satoshi last posted on the Bitcoin forum.
February 11, 2011 ~ Bitcoin was first traded against the US dollar.
June 14, 2011 ~ Wikileaks began accepting Bitcoin donations.
March 3, 2017 ~ Bitcoin was first traded against gold.
August 21, 2021 ~ First annual Bitcoin Infinity Day suggested by Knut Svanholm's meme: Everything divided by 21 million.
