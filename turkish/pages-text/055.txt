H: What Is [b]bitcoin?

[b] *Bitcoin is infinitely divisible, without debasing the
supply.*

[b] *One pizza is one pizza, regardless of how small the
slices are.*

[b] It is currently divisible to the eighth
decimal: 1.00000000

[b] There are 100,000,000 satoshis in 1 bitcoin.

[b] 1 satoshi = 0.00000001

[b] You can buy sats (satoshis) in any amount.
