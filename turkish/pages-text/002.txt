THE (NOT) GOLD STANDARD

[B] Many people still believe that money is backed by gold.

[B] It is not.

[B] It has not been backed by gold since 1971, when President Nixon unilaterally took the world off the gold standard (the Nixon Shock).

[B] See wtfhappenedin1971.com to get a clear pictureof the damage this did.
